About Myself

My name is Izak Martinez and I am a photographer. I first discovered my deep interest in photography only a few years back, but my passion in nature and traveling served as a gateway towards photography. It satisfies me to be able to capture the beauty in mother nature through an image. I strongly believe that images speak louder than words. They have such an impact on the viewers and it can be interpreted in various and unique ways to an individual. I plan to continue my passion and move on to capture the stories of the poeple on this earth through their personal pictures.
